# Homepage Composition — michaljackowski.com

## Overview

The homepage serves as the primary entry point and must accomplish several goals within seconds:

1. **Identify** — Who is Michał Jackowski?
2. **Position** — What does he do and why does it matter?
3. **Navigate** — Where can visitors find what they need?
4. **Engage** — What's current and active?

## Dynamic Content Rules

### Featured Section Logic

Items are selected based on:

1. **Priority 1:** Any item with `featured_on_homepage: true`
2. **Priority 2:** Next upcoming speaking engagement
3. **Priority 3:** Most recent publication (within last 12 months)
4. **Priority 4:** Most recent podcast episode

Maximum 3 items displayed.

### Content Freshness

- Speaking: Show upcoming first, then recent past
- Publications: Order by date, newest first
- Podcast: Always show latest episode

### Fallback Content

If no featured items:
- Display static "highlights" content
- Or hide section entirely

## Performance Considerations

### Above the Fold

Optimize for first contentful paint:
- Hero section loads first
- Photo should be optimized (WebP, appropriate size)
- No JavaScript-dependent content in hero

### Image Loading

- Hero photo: Eager load
- Pillar logos: Lazy load
- Podcast artwork: Lazy load
- Affiliation logos: Lazy load

### Content Loading

- Static content: Pre-rendered at build
- Featured items: Pre-rendered at build (or ISR)
- No client-side data fetching on homepage

## Summary

The homepage follows a clear narrative:

1. **Identity** (Hero) — Who is this?
2. **Context** (Introduction) — Why does this matter?
3. **Structure** (Pillars) — What does he do?
4. **Activity** (Featured) — What's happening now?
5. **Engagement** (Podcast) — How can I listen?
6. **Credibility** (Affiliations) — Who does he work with?
7. **Action** (Contact CTA) — How do I reach him?
8. **Navigation** (Footer) — Where else can I go?

This structure ensures visitors can quickly understand the profile while providing clear paths to deeper content based on their specific interests.
